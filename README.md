# Coffee Shop Sales Data Analysis using Excel

## Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing coffee shop sales performance across multiple store locations. The analysis covers sales trends by hour and weekday, top-selling products, footfall patterns, and category-wise distribution offering actionable insights into customer behavior and store performance.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers (Day Name, Month)

## Dashboard Features
- **Quantity Ordered Based on Hours** – Hourly trend of items ordered throughout the day
- **FootFall and Sales Over Various Store Locations** – Comparison across Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, and Lower Manhattan
- **% Size Distribution** – Breakdown of orders by size (Small, Regular, Large, Not Defined)
- **Top 5 Products Based on Sales** – Best-selling items by revenue
- **Order on Weekdays** – Daily order volume across the week
- **Categories % Distribution Based on Sales** – Sales share across Coffee, Bakery, Tea, Drinking Chocolate, and other categories

## Key Insights
- Total sales reached $698,812.33 with a footfall of 149,116 customers
- Average bill per person stood at $4.69, with an average order size of 1.44 items
- Peak ordering hours fall between 8 AM and 11 AM
- Hell's Kitchen generated the highest sales ($236,511.17) among the three store locations
- Barista Espresso was the top-selling product, generating $91,406.2 in sales
- Coffee (39%) and Bakery (28%) together make up the majority of category sales
- Friday recorded the highest order volume during the week
